WARLOCK ARC

SUPERS

Arc is a powerful subclass for warlocks, but Solar and Void are considered better. A Warlock that uses Arc is called a Stormcaller. There is only one super you can choose, but with it you will really shock your enemies. Stormtrance — Chain Arc Lightning from your hands and electrify enemies with devastating streams of Arc Light that intensify over time. This is a powerful super because over time the streams of Arc Light intensify and deal even more damage.

MELEE ABILITIES

There are two melee abilities to choose from.

Chain Lightning — This electrocuting melee ability chains lightning from the struck target to nearby enemies.

Rising Storm — This electrocuting melee ability recharges your Super, grenade, and melee energy.

GRENADES

There are also three basic grenades to choose from in this subclass.

Arcbolt Grenade — A grenade that chains bolts of lightning to nearby enemies.

Pulse Grenade — A grenade that periodically damages enemies inside its explosion radius.

Storm Grenade — A grenade that calls down a focused lightning storm.

You can visit Ikora Rey at the tower to unlock more grenades.
