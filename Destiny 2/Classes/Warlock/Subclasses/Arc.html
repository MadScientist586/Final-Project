WARLOCK ARC

Arc is a powerful subclass for warlocks, but Solar and Void are considered better. A Warlock that uses Arc is called a Stormcaller. There is only one super you can choose, but with it you will really shock your enemies. Stormtrance — Chain Arc Lightning from your hands and electrify enemies with devastating streams of Arc Light that intensify over time. This is a powerful super because over time the streams of Arc Light intensify and deal even more damage. There are three basic grenades to choose from in this subclass. Arcbolt Grenade — A grenade that chains bolts of lightning to nearby enemies. Pulse Grenade — A grenade that periodically damages enemies inside its explosion radius. Storm Grenade — A grenade that calls down a focused lightning storm. You can visit Ikora Rey at the tower to unlock more grenades.
